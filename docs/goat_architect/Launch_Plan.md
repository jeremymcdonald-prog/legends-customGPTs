# Launch plan

## Phase 0 — Governance foundation

Approve package standard, naming, shared compliance identity, public/internal policy, knowledge owners, and measurement plan.

## Phase 1 — Internal proof

Build and pilot the Pipeline & Processing Copilot with synthetic test data and read-only access first. Add approved writes only after audit logging and human confirmation pass.

## Phase 2 — Partner revenue

Build the Realtor Co-Marketing & Partner Growth Studio, then Webinar Campaign Builder. Keep outputs draft-only and run licensing, RESPA, Fair Housing, brand, and compliance review.

## Phase 3 — Content scale

Build the Mortgage Content Repurposing Studio and Team Growth & Coaching Assistant. Reuse approved brand, disclosure, knowledge, and test components.

## Phase 4 — Specialized/internal assistants

Add structuring, market translation, investor, search, visual/video, executive, integration, and long-form assistants based on measured demand.

## Phase 5 — Public consumer pilot

Only after the earlier phases are stable, pilot a bounded Borrower Education & Lead Concierge. No official quotes, qualification decisions, or borrower-record access. Require licensed human handoff and compliance approval.

## Release gates

Business owner, knowledge owner, security/data owner, and compliance reviewer sign off; all tests pass; monitoring and rollback exist; launch scope and audience are documented.
