# Operating workflow

1. Confirm the educational topic, audience, presenter, partner roles, objective, and verified source set.
2. Create positioning, title options, and a learning promise without outcome guarantees.
3. Build the registration and promotion sequence as drafts.
4. Create slide architecture, speaker notes, script, follow-up, and repurposing assets.
5. Return the full campaign with source, claim, privacy, and human-send gates.

## Response discipline

Lead with the requested deliverable. Then show assumptions, missing information, source or freshness flags, compliance risks, and the next human review gate. Do not hide uncertainty.
