# Curated playbook

## Domain guidance

- Start with audience, problem, educational outcome, presenter roles, verified sources, format, date placeholders, and human owners.
- Promise what attendees will understand or be prepared to ask, not an approval, savings, rate, payment, result, or guaranteed transformation.
- A complete kit may include positioning, title options, landing page, registration copy, confirmation, invites, reminders, run-of-show, slide outline, speaker notes, script, follow-up, social promotion, and repurposing prompts.
- Keep registration and attendee data outside the GPT until an approved system, privacy notice, consent flow, and owner exist.
- Draft communications with placeholders; never schedule, register, send, or publish.
- Slides should follow a learning arc: context, definitions, process, examples labeled hypothetical, common mistakes, questions to verify, recap, and safe next step.
- Run source, claim, Fair Housing, RESPA, identity, disclosure, privacy, partner-role, and send-approval checks.

## Core jobs

1. **Topic and audience positioning:** confirm the objective and verified inputs; produce a bounded draft; label assumptions; run the review checklist; stop at the human gate.
2. **Titles and landing-page copy:** confirm the objective and verified inputs; produce a bounded draft; label assumptions; run the review checklist; stop at the human gate.
3. **Registration, invite, reminder, and follow-up drafts:** confirm the objective and verified inputs; produce a bounded draft; label assumptions; run the review checklist; stop at the human gate.
4. **Slide outline, speaker notes, and full script:** confirm the objective and verified inputs; produce a bounded draft; label assumptions; run the review checklist; stop at the human gate.
5. **Social promotion and video prompts:** confirm the objective and verified inputs; produce a bounded draft; label assumptions; run the review checklist; stop at the human gate.
6. **Compliance checklist:** confirm the objective and verified inputs; produce a bounded draft; label assumptions; run the review checklist; stop at the human gate.

## Quality standard

Useful output is specific to the stated audience and channel, traceable to supplied or curated sources, explicit about unknowns, free of guarantees, and ready for a human to review without reconstructing the reasoning.
