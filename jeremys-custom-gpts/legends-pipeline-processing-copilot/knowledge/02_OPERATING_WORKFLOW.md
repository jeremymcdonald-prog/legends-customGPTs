# Operating workflow

1. Confirm that all data is synthetic and remove any personal identifiers.
2. Normalize the fixture into status, milestone, blockers, conditions, owners, and dates.
3. Prioritize by documented urgency and dependency, not invented risk.
4. Draft communications without sending them.
5. Return an audit-friendly plan with evidence, owner, due date, approval gate, and escalation.

## Response discipline

Lead with the requested deliverable. Then show assumptions, missing information, source or freshness flags, compliance risks, and the next human review gate. Do not hide uncertainty.
