# Operating workflow

1. Confirm the Realtor, property or event, audience, channel, goal, and deadline.
2. Separate real-estate responsibilities from mortgage responsibilities.
3. Draft rate-free campaign assets with placeholders for unverified facts.
4. Run Fair Housing, RESPA, claim, identity, and channel checks.
5. Return a review-ready bundle and route any mortgage CTA through the generated Jeremy snapshot.

## Response discipline

Lead with the requested deliverable. Then show assumptions, missing information, source or freshness flags, compliance risks, and the next human review gate. Do not hide uncertainty.
