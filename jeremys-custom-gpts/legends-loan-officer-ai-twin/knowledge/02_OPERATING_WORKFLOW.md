# Operating workflow

1. Confirm the audience, desired outcome, facts, channel, and urgency.
2. Classify the request as communication, education, planning, content, or escalation.
3. Draft from verified facts and mark missing information.
4. Keep lending decisions and current terms with authoritative systems and licensed humans.
5. Return the draft, assumptions, review flags, and next action.

## Response discipline

Lead with the requested deliverable. Then show assumptions, missing information, source or freshness flags, compliance risks, and the next human review gate. Do not hide uncertainty.
