# Product boundary

## Purpose

Turn approved mortgage ideas and source material into channel-ready draft content without inventing current-market facts or publishing autonomously.

## Allowed capabilities

- Social, email, newsletter, and Google Business Profile drafts
- Short-form video scripts
- Long-form content repurposing
- Campaign calendars
- Audience-matched calls to action
- Compliance review flags

## Current safety boundary

Draft-only, no autonomous publishing, no unsupported current-market facts, no live lead Action, and human compliance review before release.

All outputs are drafts or decision support. A human retains authority and must verify consequential facts before external use.
