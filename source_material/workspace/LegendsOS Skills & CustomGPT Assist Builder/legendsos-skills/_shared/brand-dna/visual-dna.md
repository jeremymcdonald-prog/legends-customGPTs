# LegendsOS Visual DNA

Canonical visual identity. Every skill that produces, plans, or reviews creative must reference this file.

## Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Cinematic stage | Charcoal | `#1A1A1A` |
| Deep base | Black | `#0A0A0A` |
| Energy / CTA / single key word | Loan Factory Orange | `#F26A21` |
| Luxury accent / script | Champagne Gold | `#C9A24B` |
| Headlines | White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Support type | Silver | `#C7C7C7` |

One accent leads per image. Orange OR gold, not both fighting.

## Typography

Max two type families per image plus the official logo lockup.

| Level | Role | Style | Color |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary Headline | The one big idea | Heavy condensed sans (Anton / Archivo Black), uppercase or strong title case | White, orange on the single key word |
| Secondary Hook | One support line | Medium sans (Montserrat SemiBold) | Silver / white |
| Premium Accent | Brand moments ("Momentum," "Closing Day") | Elegant script or high-contrast serif | Champagne gold |
| Proof Line | Stat, credential, sub-benefit | Small caps sans | Orange or silver |
| CTA | Action | Bold sans in an orange chip or underline | White on orange, or orange on dark |
| Compliance Footer | NMLS, EHO, disclaimers | Clean small sans, readable, never decorative | Silver / light gray on dark |
| Logo Lockup | Legends + Powered by Loan Factory | Official lockup only | As provided |

## Feel

Premium mortgage command center. Cinematic realism, not cartoon, not stock-flyer. Clean directional lighting on the face. Strong face presence. Luxury but not flashy. Bold but compliant.

## Texture

Subtle film grain, soft bokeh, controlled lens flare on orange. No heavy gradients, no rainbow, no clip-art icons.

## Three Engines

- **Cinematic Authority** — trust, leadership, premium recognition. Dark luxury, subject centered, gold accents, minimal text.
- **Pattern Interrupt** — stop the scroll with emotion, contrast, tension. High contrast, single charged emotion, orange energy. Use sparingly. Never name or depict a real competitor.
- **Educational Minimal** — teach one mortgage idea fast. One color-blocked text zone, one big idea, breathing room, mobile-first.

## What this DNA replaces

White backgrounds, circular headshot footers, crowded copy blocks, cartoon mascots, chrome word-art logos, named-competitor gags.

## Do Not Create

- generic Canva flyers
- cluttered mortgage ads
- tiny unreadable compliance text
- fake rates
- fake approvals
- fake testimonials
- cheap stock broker imagery
- overly AI-looking faces
- cartoon mortgage graphics
- flat white background templates
- random typography systems
- generic corporate banking style

## Mobile safe zones

Keep all text and the face inside the center 80 percent. Nothing critical in the outer 10 percent on any edge. Assume the bottom 12 percent may be covered by platform UI.

## Core production rule

AI builds the cinematic plate. Canva adds the words and the compliance. Never instruct an image model to render headlines, CTAs, logos, or legal text. Always reserve a clean text zone and a bottom compliance strip for Canva.
