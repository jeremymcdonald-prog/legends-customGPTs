# Layer 3 — Asset Prompt Reference

Per-format direction. Aspect ratio, subject placement, motion notes.

## Platform specs

| Format | Aspect | Pixels | Subject position | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Facebook square | 1:1 | 1080x1080 | Right third | Left two-thirds clean for headline; bottom strip reserved |
| Instagram square | 1:1 | 1080x1080 | Right third | Face in center 80% safe zone |
| Instagram story | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | Lower or center | Top 25% clean for headline, bottom 15% for CTA + compliance; outer 10% top/bottom for story UI |
| Reel cover | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | Center | Survives 1:1 crop; clean band for 3-4 word hook |
| YouTube Shorts cover | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | One dominant subject | Generous clean zone for 3-4 word hook |
| YouTube thumbnail | 16:9 | 1280x720 | One side | Other side clean for 3-5 word headline; reads at small size |
| LinkedIn post | 1:1 or 1.91:1 | 1080x1080 / 1200x628 | Premium office | Slightly more corporate, still dark luxury |
| Google Business Profile | 1:1 or 4:3 | 1200x900 | Subject right | NE FL feel when relevant; bottom strip reserved |
| Webinar promo | 16:9 or 1:1 | 1920x1080 / 1080x1080 | Right third | Dark stage left for title block, gold topic accent, date/time chip zone |
| Email header banner | ~3:1 | 1200x400 | Optional side | Wide center-left zone for headline; full lockup + thin compliance line |

## Motion (when generating video)

| Motion type | Use for | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Push-in | Reels/Shorts hooks | 1-2s |
| Subject turn | Authority openers | 1-2s |
| Parallax | Title plate intros | 2-3s |
| Logo reveal | Webinar / authority intros | 2-3s |
| Dashboard glow | AI Authority B-roll | 3-5s |

## Universal asset rules

- Mobile safe zone: center 80% safe, outer 10% margin, bottom 12% reserved for compliance.
- Text zones reserved, never rendered by AI.
- Logo lockup placement: bottom-left or bottom-center per layout.
- Compliance strip: bottom 10-12%, legible at phone size.
