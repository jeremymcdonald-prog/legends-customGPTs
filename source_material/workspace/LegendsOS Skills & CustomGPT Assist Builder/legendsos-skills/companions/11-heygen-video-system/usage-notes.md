# Usage Notes — HeyGen Video System

## How to invoke

- "Build a HeyGen short on [topic] in [persona]."
- "Spec a HeyGen long form on [topic]."
- "Plan the HeyGen batch for Week N."

## What you get back

A header, a full HeyGen prompt block, a thumbnail spec paired with a Canva template ID, compliance footer if mortgage-related, the production tracker row, and one next step.

## Two pacing modes

- **Short.** 25 to 60 seconds. One idea. One CTA. Designed for Reel / TikTok / YT Short.
- **Long.** 6 to 12 minutes. Educational chapters. Designed for YouTube Long Form, sometimes blog-paired.

## TTS tips that matter

- Short sentences. One idea per line.
- Use line breaks where you'd be tempted to use em dashes.
- Spell out small numbers ("five percent" not "5%").
- Use digits for years and rates context ("2026").
- Phonetic spellings only when HeyGen pronounces poorly (e.g., "D S C R" with spaces).

## Pairing with the Story Bank

Story videos require a source ID. The skill refuses to write a story script without one.

## Pairing with Canva

Every HeyGen video gets a paired Canva thumbnail template. The skill outputs the template ID and the thumbnail headline.

## Pairing with Higgsfield

When B-roll needs a cinematic AI plate (a Florida lifestyle scene, a luxury closing scene, a market-volatility metaphor), the skill names the Higgsfield asset ID and points to `companions/12-higgsfield-visual-system`.
