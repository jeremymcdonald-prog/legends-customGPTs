# Script Formats

The library of repeatable script formats this skill draws from.

## 1. Daily short (Coach or Inside Man)

- Length: 30 to 45 seconds.
- Structure: Hook → Setup → Body (3 beats) → Land → CTA.
- Use for: daily education, broker advantage, single AI tip.

## 2. Mortgage story short (Coach)

- Length: 35 to 55 seconds.
- Structure: Hook (the dramatic setup) → Stakes → Move → Result → Lesson → CTA.
- Use for: anonymized borrower outcomes.

## 3. Realtor AI tip short (Inside Man)

- Length: 30 to 45 seconds.
- Structure: Hook (the time savings) → The Move (3 steps) → Two Rules → Lesson → CTA.
- Use for: Realtor-facing AI tips.

## 4. Closing day video (Jeremy on camera preferred, HeyGen acceptable)

- Length: 25 to 40 seconds.
- Structure: Reveal (closing day with city) → Lesson → Thanks → CTA.
- VA variant adds the non-endorsement line.

## 5. VA buyer video (Coach)

- Length: 40 to 60 seconds.
- Structure: Hook (VA benefit reality) → Three things → Lesson → CTA.
- Always includes VA non-endorsement line.

## 6. Broker advantage video (Coach)

- Length: 40 to 60 seconds.
- Structure: Setup (the false equivalence) → Contrast (broker vs retail) → Mechanic (240+ lenders, flat fee) → Land → CTA.
- Never names a competitor.

## 7. YouTube long form (Inside Man)

- Length: 6 to 12 minutes.
- Structure: Cold open (60s hook) → Promise → Chapter 1 → Chapter 2 → Chapter 3 → Chapter 4 → Q&A → CTA + next video.
- Each chapter 60 to 120 seconds.
- Includes B-roll cues for at least 3 cinematic plates.

## 8. Weekly recap (Wolf or Coach)

- Length: 90 to 180 seconds.
- Structure: Headline of the week → Rate move (no APR-less quote) → Story of the week (anonymized) → AI tip of the week → CTA.

## 9. Personal brand short (Jeremy on camera preferred, HeyGen acceptable)

- Length: 25 to 45 seconds.
- Structure: Moment → Reflection → Lesson → No CTA or soft CTA.

## 10. Market update (Wolf)

- Length: 45 to 75 seconds.
- Structure: Headline reality vs perception → Bond move context → Buyer implication → No APR-less rate quote → CTA.

## Selection rule

The skill picks the format that fits the topic. Format selection is documented in the prompt block header.
