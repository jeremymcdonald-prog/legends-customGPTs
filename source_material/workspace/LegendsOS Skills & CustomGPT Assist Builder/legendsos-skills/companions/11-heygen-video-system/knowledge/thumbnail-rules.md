# Thumbnail Rules

Every HeyGen video gets a paired thumbnail. The skill emits the spec; the Canva Template System fills it.

## Canva templates for thumbnails

| Format | Template |
|---|---|
| YouTube Long Form | `TMPL_YT_LONG_THUMB_v1` |
| YouTube Short | `TMPL_YT_SHORT_v1` |
| Instagram Reel cover | `TMPL_REEL_COVER_v1` |
| TikTok cover (if used) | `TMPL_REEL_COVER_v1` (same canvas) |

## Headline rules

- 3 to 4 words for YT Long.
- 3 to 5 words for YT Short and Reel cover.
- Sentence case or strong title case.
- One accent word in orange OR gold (per Visual DNA).

## Face presence

- YT Long thumbnails: Jeremy's face required, large, single emotion.
- YT Short and Reel covers: face optional, often image plate.

## Forbidden moves

- Stock photo "shocked guy" thumbnail style.
- Arrow + circle clickbait.
- Three-stack text walls.
- Tiny text under 80px at 1280x720.
- Fake "BREAKING" banner.

## Examples

| Video topic | Thumbnail headline | Accent |
|---|---|---|
| Broker advantage | "BROKERS SHOP. BANKS SELL." | BROKERS |
| Closing day VA | "ZERO DOWN. NO PMI." | ZERO |
| DSCR long form | "DSCR REAL TALK" | REAL |
| Realtor AI tip | "5 MIN LISTING" | 5 |
| Market update | "RATES MOVED." | MOVED |
| Personal brand | "4:45 AM." | 4:45 |

## A/B option

The skill emits one primary thumbnail headline and one alternate, for the operator to A/B if desired.
