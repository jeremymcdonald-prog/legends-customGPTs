# Compliance — HeyGen Video System

Reference `_shared/brand-dna/compliance-pack.md`. Skill-specific:

## Hard blocks

- Rate quote without APR and assumptions.
- Guarantee dollar amount without verified config.
- Named customer without written permission.
- Address paired with named customer.
- Competitor name on camera or in script.
- Fake testimonial.

## Required additions

- VA video → VA non-endorsement line in caption + optionally on-screen.
- FHA video → FHA program guidelines line in caption.
- USDA video → USDA eligibility line in caption.
- DSCR video → business-purpose line in caption.
- Guarantee video → full guarantee disclaimer block + "All states except Washington."

## Avatar identity rule

HeyGen avatar of Jeremy is allowed because Jeremy authorizes his own likeness. Avatars of other named individuals are not permitted unless that person has written authorization on file.

## TTS compliance line cadence

- Avoid running disclaimers on the visual; put them in the description.
- If the disclaimer must be on-screen (guarantee video), use small lower-third silver text on the last 4 seconds.
