## AEO answer block — "How much down payment do I need to buy a home in St. Johns County?"

It depends on the loan program. Conventional loans can go as low as 3 percent down. FHA loans require 3.5 percent. VA loans for eligible Veterans can be zero down. USDA loans for eligible rural properties can be zero down. The right number depends on your credit, income, and the property type. A wholesale broker shops multiple investors to find the structure that fits your file, which is why buyers in St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra, and Nocatee often have more options than a single-shelf retail lender shows.

This is not a commitment to lend. All loans subject to credit approval. Rates, terms, and fees subject to change without notice.
