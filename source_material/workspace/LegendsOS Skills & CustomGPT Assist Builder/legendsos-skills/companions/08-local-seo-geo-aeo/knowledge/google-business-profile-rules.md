# Google Business Profile Rules

## Post structure

| Element | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Length | 80 to 250 words |
| Local angle | One. City or county mentioned naturally. |
| CTA | One button. Call / Learn More / Sign Up / Book. |
| Image | One Canva-produced image (see Canva Template System TMPL_GBP_*). |
| NMLS + EHO | In the body. |
| Footer | Full footer block at the end. |
| Keyword density | Max two natural mentions of the target city or county. |

## Tone

- Plain spoken.
- No fluff.
- Local without being a tourist brochure.
- Sounds like Jeremy explaining at a Realtor lunch, not a regional bank press release.

## Posting cadence

3 to 5 posts per week. Educational + program update + service offer mix.

## Topic categories

| Category | What it does |
|---|---|
| Local education | Teaches a mortgage idea grounded in Northeast Florida context |
| Program update | Highlights VA, FHA, USDA, DSCR, FTHB programs for the area |
| Service offer | Soft offer, never a hard sell, with one CTA |
| Story-backed lesson | Pulled from Story Bank, anonymized |
| Market context | When real, sourced market data is available |
| Holiday or event | Local Jacksonville event, Veterans Day, tax season |

## Review reply rules

- Reply within 24 to 72 hours.
- 30 to 80 words.
- Warm, never effusive.
- Thank the reviewer by their public name only.
- Do not reference loan terms, rate, payment.
- Do not invite further public details.
- Include the soft mention of Northeast Florida service area.
- Sign with NMLS attribution.

## What never goes on GBP

- Rate quotes without APR.
- Guarantee dollar amount on the image without verified live config.
- Competitor mentions.
- Customer photos without written approval.
- Address-pairing with named customers.
- Stacked CTAs.
- Phrases like "lowest rates in Jacksonville."
