# Local Content Angles

The angles that work for Northeast Florida mortgage content.

## Angles that win

- "Moving to [city]" buyer perspective (out-of-state buyer prep).
- Veteran-focused content for the heavy military presence (NAS Jacksonville, NS Mayport, NSB Kings Bay nearby).
- First time buyer pathway in St. Johns County (good public schools, family draw).
- Investor angle in Jacksonville core neighborhoods (rental demand).
- New construction angle in Nocatee, World Golf Village, Yulee.
- Coastal lifestyle angle in Jacksonville Beach, Ponte Vedra, Atlantic Beach.
- Refinance angle when rates move.

## Angles to avoid

- Generic "best neighborhoods in Jacksonville" without real value (low intent, high competition).
- "Top 10 schools in [county]" content (we are mortgage, not education media).
- Generic stock photo posts about house keys with no local relevance.

## Local trust signals to weave in

- 240+ wholesale lenders through Loan Factory.
- Northeast Florida focus.
- Broker access vs single-shelf retail.
- Real closing experience volume across the region.
- Realtor partner network locally.
