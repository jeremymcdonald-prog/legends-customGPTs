# Usage Notes — Local SEO

## When to invoke

- "Write a GBP post about X for Y city."
- "Plan a blog post for VA Veterans in St. Johns County."
- "Build an AEO answer for 'best mortgage broker in Jacksonville.'"
- "What should our next month of GBP posts cover?"

## Defaults

- Market: Jacksonville and Northeast Florida.
- Surface: GBP unless specified.
- Tone: Jeremy's voice.
- CTA: from the safe CTA library, biased toward call or learn more for GBP.

## What you get back

The canonical local piece, the schema recommendation, the internal link plan, the social derivative routing note, the compliance footer, the production tracker row, and the next step.

## Pairing with Story Bank

If a local piece is review-backed:

1. Pull a source ID from the Story Bank.
2. Include it in the header.
3. Use lesson-frame language, not testimonial repost (unless source is verified public review).

## Pairing with Social Media

The Local SEO skill writes the canonical local piece. The Social Media skill writes the platform-native derivatives. The two skills agree on the seed and the CTA.

## What this skill does not do

- Auto-publish to GBP.
- Deploy schema to the website.
- Scrape reviews.
- Invent local stats.
