# Compliance — Local SEO, GEO, AEO, GBP

Reference `_shared/brand-dna/compliance-pack.md`. Skill-specific:

## Hard blocks

- No invented local statistics. If a stat is needed, it requires a cited source.
- No "lowest rate," "best rate," "lowest fees," "best mortgage broker" used as bare superlatives.
- No "Jeremy is #1 in Jacksonville" without verified, sourced, current ranking.
- No "as featured in" claims without verified sourcing.
- No competitor lender names in local copy.
- NAP must exactly match `_shared/knowledge/service-area-nap.md`. Do not invent or guess.
- Multi-state guarantee content carries "All states except Washington."
- Guarantee dollar amounts only with verified current config.

## GBP rules (paste into compliance gate)

- One local angle per post.
- One CTA per post.
- NMLS and Equal Housing in the body of the post.
- No keyword stuffing. Maximum two natural mentions of the target city or county.
- No fake reviews or manipulated testimonial language.
- Review replies follow the GBP review reply rules (see `knowledge/google-business-profile-rules.md`).

## AEO blocks

- AEO answer blocks are 40 to 80 words, plain English, direct answer first, then context.
- Cited sources when stats are used.
- No promise language ("we guarantee," "you'll save").

## Blog and vlog

- Every blog post has a clear Q-and-A or H2 structure that supports featured snippets.
- Every blog post has an internal link plan back to service pages.
- Every vlog page includes transcript text on the page for AEO.
