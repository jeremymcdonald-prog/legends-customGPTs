# Brand Layouts

The four canonical layouts every template draws from.

## Layout A — Hero Headline + Footer

Use: closing day, motivational, broker advantage single, GBP simple.

- Top 20%: brand/logo lockup or breathing space
- Middle 60%: headline + accent word + optional sub
- Bottom 20%: footer (NMLS, EHO, location label)

## Layout B — Split (Image Plate + Text Block)

Use: lifestyle, Realtor AI tip with B-roll feel, personal brand.

- Left or top 50%: image plate (Higgsfield or photo)
- Right or bottom 50%: text block (headline + 2 to 3 lines + CTA)
- Footer strip overlays the bottom of the canvas

## Layout C — Carousel Sequence

Use: education carousels.

- Slide 1: hook, hero headline
- Slides 2 to N-2: one idea per slide, accent word per slide
- Slide N-1: takeaway
- Slide N: CTA + footer

Color rotation: alternate slides between charcoal and black for rhythm. Accent word color rotates between orange and gold (one per slide, not both).

## Layout D — Story Frame Sequence

Use: IG Stories.

- 4 or 5 frames vertical 1080x1920
- Frame 1: hook with biggest text
- Frames 2 to N-1: short payoff lines
- Frame N: CTA + footer strip with NMLS + EHO

## Reserved zones rule

UI overlay on Reels and Stories sits at the bottom 18%. Critical text must clear that zone.

## Headline length guidance

| Layout | Headline limit |
|---|---|
| A | 7 words |
| B | 6 words |
| C (slide 1) | 5 words |
| C (other slides) | 8 words |
| D (frame 1) | 4 words |
| D (other frames) | 6 words |

## Logo lockup

Use the official Legends + Powered by Loan Factory lockup only. Position depends on layout:

- Layout A: top center or top right corner.
- Layout B: corner of the text block.
- Layout C: slide 1 and slide N.
- Layout D: last frame only.
