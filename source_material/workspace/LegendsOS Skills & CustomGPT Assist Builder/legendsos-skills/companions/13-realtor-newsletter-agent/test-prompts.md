## Test 1 — Format 1: Weekly Realtor AI Advantage Newsletter

**Prompt:**
Generate a Format 1 Weekly Realtor AI Advantage Newsletter.

Topic: How Realtors can use AI to follow up with open house leads faster and qualify buyers before the second showing.
Audience: Buyer agents in Jacksonville and St. Johns County.
Local focus: Nocatee and Ponte Vedra.
Loan programs: Conventional, FHA, VA.
CTA: Reply to this email to get the follow up script we use.
Output: Full newsletter plus subject line options and preview text.

**What to check:**
- Voice is plain and direct, not corporate
- No emojis, no em dashes
- No rate claims or guaranteed approval language
- AI tip is practical and specific
- CTA is clear and low friction
- Compliance footer is included
- Any AI Realtor Pro reference is flagged [VERIFY]

## Test 5 — Social Repurposing Only

**Prompt:**
Take this newsletter headline and repurpose it for Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram.

Headline: The buyer lost the house. The loan wasn't the problem. The structure was.

Topic context: A buyer was pre-approved but the offer fell apart because the debt-to-income ratio changed after they bought a car before closing.
Audience: Realtors in Jacksonville.
Do not include rate claims, guaranteed approval language, or specific borrower details.
Output: Facebook post, LinkedIn post, Instagram caption.

**What to check:**
- Facebook version has a local or story angle and a question or hook
- LinkedIn version leads with professional insight
- Instagram caption is under 150 words and not hashtag-heavy
- None of the three versions includes compliance violations
- Each version feels native to the platform, not copy-pasted
