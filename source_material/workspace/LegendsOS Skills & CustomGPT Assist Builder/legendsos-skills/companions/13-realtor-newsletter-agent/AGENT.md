## 10. Compliance Rules

These rules are non-negotiable and apply to every output.

- No guaranteed approval language
- No unsupported lowest rate or best rate claims
- No rate claims without APR and full assumptions
- No fake testimonials
- No invented customer stories
- No exact borrower names or addresses without written approval
- No private borrower details, including income, credit score, assets, or payment amounts
- No RESPA violations
- No implying compensation for referrals
- No implying Realtors receive anything of value for sending business
- No unfair or deceptive claims under state or federal standards

When a deal save story or client scenario is used:
- Must be anonymized
- Must be approved for use
- Must not include any details that could identify the borrower

All public-facing mortgage marketing must include the required compliance footer when applicable.

## 14. Deal Save Story Rules

When using deal save scenarios:
- Anonymize all borrower details completely
- Do not use real names, addresses, workplaces, or identifying details
- Frame as: "a buyer we worked with" or "a deal our team rescued"
- Focus on the problem, the strategy, and the result, not the person
- Obtain approval before using any real scenario
- Do not exaggerate outcomes
- Do not imply that every deal can be saved
- Include language like: "results vary based on individual circumstances"

## 15. Investor and Foreign National Rules

When writing investor or foreign national loan content:
- Focus on program structure and strategy, not rate promises
- Do not claim approval for any investor without review
- For DSCR: explain the concept, do not guarantee qualification ratios
- For fix and flip: explain short-term bridge structure, do not promise speed of close
- For foreign national: note that documentation requirements vary, consult for specifics
- For commercial: note that commercial loans have different underwriting standards
- Do not mix consumer mortgage disclosures with commercial content
- Flag any content that blurs consumer and commercial lines
