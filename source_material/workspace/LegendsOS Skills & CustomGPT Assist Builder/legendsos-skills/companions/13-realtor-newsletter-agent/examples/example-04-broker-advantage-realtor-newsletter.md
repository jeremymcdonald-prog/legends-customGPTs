## Newsletter Body

Most Realtors are taught early that the safe move is to send a buyer to a big bank. Same logo on the building. Familiar name. It feels low risk. But after 1,600 plus closings I can tell you that defaulting to a bank is the single biggest reason buyers feel stuck and Realtors lose deals to indecision.

### Quick Market Context

A bank or retail lender shops one menu, their own. A mortgage broker shops dozens of wholesale lenders to find structure and pricing the buyer cannot see from inside one institution. In a market where buyers are sensitive to every dollar of monthly payment, that difference is the deal.

### Mortgage Strategy

I am a broker, not a bank. I shop multiple wholesale lenders so your buyer gets better pricing, fewer surprises, and a cleaner closing. Verbatim from our website, "I shop 90-230+ wholesale lenders instead of one bank menu." That is not a marketing line. That is a different cost structure for your buyer.

Three differences that matter to a Realtor.

1. **Lender fees.** Most banks stack 2,500 dollars or more in lender costs. The Legends Mortgage Team charges 0 dollars in lender fees.
2. **Program range.** Conventional, FHA, VA, USDA, jumbo, DSCR, fix and flip, ground up investor construction, FHA 203k, HomeReady, Home Possible, ITIN, bank statement, HELOCs for primary and investment. A bank rarely runs even half of that menu.
3. **Loan officer access.** You work with me directly from strategy through closing. Not an 800 number. Not a different rep every week. Loan Factory's price guarantee program is also available on eligible loans [VERIFY GUARANTEE LANGUAGE, AMOUNT, AND TERMS BEFORE PUBLISHING].

### Deal Save Lesson

Here is the pattern. A buyer gets a quote from their bank. They tell their agent, "I am pre-approved." The Realtor moves them into showings. Two weeks later, the file slows. Documents stack. The bank pulls a structure that worked for the lender, not the buyer. The buyer walks because the payment now does not match what they were told.

A broker who shops the market would have caught the structure mismatch in week one and saved the deal before it ever became one.

### Realtor Talking Point

You can use this with any buyer who says, "I am just going to go through my bank."

"I work with a broker, not because I am loyal to him, but because he shops 90 plus lenders for you. If your bank still wins the comparison, that is great. But you owe yourself the comparison before you tie 30 years of payments to one menu."

That sentence does not insult anyone. It just protects the buyer.
