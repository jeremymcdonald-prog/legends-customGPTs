## Newsletter Body

If you are a listing agent in Jacksonville, your time is the most expensive thing in your business. So instead of writing about ideas you cannot use this week, I want to walk you through two things our team has put on the table that you can use right now.

A single new listing can become a week of social content if you handle it like a campaign instead of a single post. Hero photo. Walkthrough. Neighborhood angle. School and lifestyle context. Buyer agent CTA. Open house teaser. Recap.

Most agents post once and move on. The agents who win in 2026 are the ones who turn one listing into ten touchpoints across email, Instagram, Facebook, video, and SMS, then run that on every listing automatically.
