### Listing Content Opportunity

One new listing is not one post. It is a campaign. Done right, a single listing should produce.

- A hero photo carousel for Instagram and Facebook.
- A walkthrough video in a 30 second short and a long form version.
- A neighborhood and lifestyle angle for the local audience.
- A buyer email with the payment range, not just the price.
- An open house teaser and a recap post.
- A YouTube long form when the property earns it.

Most agents post one of those. The agents with AI in their workflow run all of them.

### Open House Follow Up Idea

The 48 hour window after an open house is where most deals are quietly lost. A good AI workflow lets you send a thank you within an hour, a soft mortgage check-in by the next morning, and a real one to one invitation 24 hours later. You spent the time hosting the open house. AI keeps that work earning past Sunday night.
