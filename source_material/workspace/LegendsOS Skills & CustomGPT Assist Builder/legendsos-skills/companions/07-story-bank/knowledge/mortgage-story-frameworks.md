# Mortgage Story Frameworks

The eleven frameworks every story gets mapped to. The Social Media skill, the Local SEO skill, and the HeyGen Video skill all pull by framework when planning a week.

## 1. The File Save

**Setup.** A buyer's file was about to die at a lender.
**Move.** A different wholesale investor, a different overlay, a different structure saved the file.
**Lesson.** Broker access wins where retail loses.
**Tags.** broker_advantage, problem_solving.
**Default risk.** Yellow.

## 2. The Veteran Win

**Setup.** A Veteran family was navigating their benefit for the first time, or had been told no.
**Move.** Proper VA structuring, the right investor for the file.
**Lesson.** Not every lender treats VA the same. The benefit only works if the lender knows how to use it.
**Tags.** va, veteran, st_johns, duval, clay, nassau.
**Default risk.** Yellow. VA non-endorsement line required.

## 3. The First Time Buyer Confidence Shift

**Setup.** A buyer thought they were not ready. Felt overwhelmed.
**Move.** Clear math, real options, broker patience.
**Lesson.** Buying a home is doable when someone takes the time to teach the math.
**Tags.** first_time_buyer, education.
**Default risk.** Yellow.

## 4. The Realtor Rescue

**Setup.** A Realtor's deal was at risk because the original lender slowed down.
**Move.** We took the file, restructured, hit the close date.
**Lesson.** Realtors who bring us in earlier keep more deals.
**Tags.** realtor, broker_advantage.
**Default risk.** Yellow.

## 5. The Payment Reality Check

**Setup.** A buyer fixated on a rate and ignored the payment math.
**Move.** Walked through the real total picture (P&I, taxes, insurance, structure choice).
**Lesson.** The rate is one of seven things that matter. Payment math wins.
**Tags.** education, affordability.
**Default risk.** Yellow. No specific rate or payment quoted.

## 6. The Broker Advantage Story

**Setup.** A buyer or Realtor was about to default to a bank or retail lender.
**Move.** Shopped the wholesale field, found a better fit.
**Lesson.** Brokers shop. Banks sell. Different shelf, different result.
**Tags.** broker_advantage, wholesale, loan_factory.
**Default risk.** Yellow.

## 7. The Investor Numbers Story

**Setup.** A real estate investor analyzing a Florida property.
**Move.** Right loan product (DSCR, conventional investor, bridge, hard money) for the deal's profile.
**Lesson.** Loan product fit is half the deal math.
**Tags.** investor, dscr, business_purpose, florida.
**Default risk.** Yellow. Business-purpose line required.

## 8. The Move Up Buyer Strategy Story

**Setup.** A current homeowner wanting to move up without losing their equity position.
**Move.** Bridge structure, contingency timing, or rate-and-term planning.
**Lesson.** Move up buyers have more levers than they think.
**Tags.** move_up, equity, strategy.
**Default risk.** Yellow.

## 9. The Appraisal or Inspection Curveball Story

**Setup.** Appraisal came in low. Inspection raised a flag. Deal at risk.
**Move.** Negotiation between agents, investor flexibility, or a creative restructure.
**Lesson.** The broker who stays in the deal saves the deal.
**Tags.** appraisal, inspection, problem_solving.
**Default risk.** Yellow.

## 10. The Closing Day Lesson

**Setup.** Closing day went smoothly because of something done weeks earlier.
**Move.** The early discipline that prevented the late chaos.
**Lesson.** Closing day is downstream of week one decisions.
**Tags.** closing_day, process.
**Default risk.** Yellow.

## 11. The AI Helped the Realtor Win Story

**Setup.** A Realtor used an AI workflow (we taught them) to win a listing, retarget a buyer, or move faster than competitors.
**Move.** Concrete AI step the Realtor took (Claude prompt, ChatGPT workflow, automated follow-up).
**Lesson.** AI is not coming. It is here. The Realtors who use it well will outpace the ones who do not.
**Tags.** realtor, ai, training.
**Default risk.** Green (when no mortgage data is referenced).

## Framework selection rules

- The skill picks the framework that best fits the raw source.
- If two frameworks fit, pick the more specific one and tag both.
- Frameworks are not exclusive; one source can power multiple posts across multiple frameworks over time.
