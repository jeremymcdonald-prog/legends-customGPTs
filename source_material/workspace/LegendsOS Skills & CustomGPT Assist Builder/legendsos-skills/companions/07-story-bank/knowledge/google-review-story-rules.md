# Google Review Story Rules

## Source

Google Business Profile reviews for Jeremy McDonald and The Legends Mortgage Team.

Search index reference: https://www.google.com/search?q=jeremy+mcdonald+mortgage+broker

Operator pastes the review text and the GBP review link in.

## Source ID

`GBP_REVIEW_SOURCE_xxx`

## Public treatment

- `source_url` required (GBP review URL or GBP profile URL).
- Reframe is the default.
- Word-for-word quote allowed only with the citation "Public review on Google" included in the post.
- Star rating may be referenced ("5-star review") but not paired with a rate or savings claim.

## Anonymization

- Reviewer's first name visible on Google may be used in a public-quote post.
- Last name typically not shown; do not invent it.
- Do not pair reviewer with an address.

## Output bias

Stories sourced from Google reviews lean toward:

- Local trust (Jacksonville, Northeast Florida)
- Communication quality
- Closing experience
- "I would refer Jeremy to anyone" repeat-business signals

## Forbidden

- No bulk re-publication of reviews into a single post that reads like a paid testimonial wall.
- No editing of review text to make it more flattering. Reframe yes. Rewrite no.
- No combining a review reframe with a rate or savings number in the same image.

## GBP post path

GBP review-based stories often become GBP posts. The local angle is required, and the Local SEO skill is the natural downstream.
