## Repurposing

A single source ID typically powers:

- 1 long form (YT Long, blog) over time
- 2 to 3 short forms (Reel, TikTok, YT Short)
- 1 carousel (IG Feed)
- 2 to 4 single posts (FB Personal, FB Business, LinkedIn, GBP)

That is 6 to 9 pieces of content per source over a 60-day window.
