# Motion Visual Rules

## What it is

A short looping motion piece used as a background, intro, or transition layer.

## Use cases

- YT Long form intro background (8 to 15 seconds).
- YT Long form outro background.
- Stream-style banner motion for Reels.
- Carousel transition (rare, only when justified).

## Spec rules

- Duration 8 to 15 seconds.
- Looping seam clean.
- Low complexity. Background, not foreground.
- Palette: dark luxury (charcoal + accent).
- Text-safe zones held throughout the motion.
- Output format: mp4 (1080x1920 vertical or 1920x1080 horizontal).

## Motion vocabulary

- Slow drift (clouds, particles, ambient light).
- Gentle camera push (1 to 2% scale change).
- Subtle particle motion (dust, light flares).
- Light pulse (one accent light brightens / dims).

## Forbidden

- Strobing.
- Rapid color shifts.
- Type animation (text lives in Canva or final cut layer).
- High-frequency motion that distracts.

## When to skip motion and stay still

- The asset's primary job is to hold text.
- The watch time is short and motion is more distraction than enhancement.
- Render budget is tight.
