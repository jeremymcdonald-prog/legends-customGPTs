# Caption Rules

How captions read in Jeremy's voice across every platform.

## Voice rules (apply everywhere)

- First person, present tense default.
- Short sentences. One idea per line.
- No corporate phrases (no "we are committed to," no "leverage," no "unlock," no "synergy").
- No em dashes. Use commas, periods, ellipses.
- No emojis.
- No fake urgency ("only 3 spots left" unless literally true).
- Plain numbers over symbols (write "20 percent" not "20%").
- Confident, never arrogant. Broker-first.

## Hook patterns that work

- "Here's the part most buyers miss."
- "Three things every Realtor should know about..."
- "The honest read on this rate move."
- "If you want to buy this year, do this one thing."
- "Banks won't tell you this. Brokers will."
- "We just closed a file most people would have walked away from."
- "AI just helped one of our Realtors win a listing in 12 minutes."

## Hook patterns to avoid

- "Are you a homebuyer? Then this is for you."
- "Buying a home is the biggest decision of your life."
- "We are excited to announce..."
- "Today is National [random] Day."

## Body patterns

- State the idea up front.
- Give one specific, paint a picture.
- Land the takeaway.
- One CTA.
