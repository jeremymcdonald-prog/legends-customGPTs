# Channel Strategy

Per-channel role inside The Legends Mortgage Team's social system. This is the strategic map; tactical rules live in `platform-output-rules.md`.

## Facebook Personal

**Audience.** Friends, family, sphere, past clients, Jacksonville locals who already know Jeremy.
**Role.** Trust amplifier. Personal brand and personal wins. Light mortgage education tucked between human posts.
**Mix per week.** 4 personal or lifestyle, 2 closing wins or stories, 1 light education.
**Voice tilt.** Most personal. Family, gym, faith, travel, leadership, real moments.

## Facebook Business

**Audience.** Local search audience, Jacksonville Realtors, past clients, referral partners.
**Role.** The local broker brand of record. Reviews, closings, market notes, broker advantage, GBP-style local content.
**Mix per week.** 3 broker advantage or education, 2 anonymized stories or wins, 2 Realtor or local content.
**Voice tilt.** Confident broker authority, plain spoken.

## Instagram Feed

**Audience.** Mixed local plus mortgage and Realtor industry.
**Role.** Visual education. Carousels and single-image posts. Authority through clean visuals.
**Mix per week.** 3 carousels, 2 single image education, 2 closing or story posts.
**Voice tilt.** Tight captions, visual-first.

## Instagram Reels

**Audience.** Algorithmic discovery, buyers, Realtors, agents nationwide who study mortgage content.
**Role.** Top-of-funnel reach. Mortgage stories, AI tips, broker advantage, market commentary.
**Mix per week.** 5 to 7 Reels. At least 1 mortgage story, 1 Realtor AI tip, 1 broker advantage, 1 market or program education, 1 personal brand or motivation.

## Instagram Stories

**Audience.** Existing followers, sphere, partners.
**Role.** Daily presence. Behind the scenes, polls, quick wins, link forwards, repost frames for Reels and Feed.
**Mix per day.** 1 morning, 1 evening minimum. More on closing days.

## TikTok

**Audience.** Younger first-time buyer pool, Realtor and LO industry, AI-curious creators.
**Role.** Pattern interrupt education. Faster, more raw than Reels.
**Mix per week.** 5 to 7 posts. Repurpose Reels with a TikTok-native hook and on-screen text.

## YouTube Shorts

**Audience.** Search-driven, evergreen.
**Role.** Search and evergreen mortgage education that compounds.
**Mix per week.** 5 to 7. Use the same Reel script repurposed for the YouTube hook style.

## YouTube Long Form

**Audience.** Search-driven, decision-stage buyers, Realtors looking to learn.
**Role.** Authority. Deep dives, full program walk-throughs, market explainers, Realtor AI training.
**Mix per week.** 2 long form pieces, Tuesday and Thursday default.

## LinkedIn

**Audience.** Industry, recruits, Loan Factory peers, real estate professionals, business owners.
**Role.** Leadership and authority. CTO positioning. AI Implementation lens. Team building.
**Mix per week.** 5 posts. 2 leadership or strategy, 1 AI implementation lens, 1 industry commentary, 1 team or culture.

## Google Business Profile

**Audience.** Local search, AI answer engines.
**Role.** Local SEO and AEO. Service-area authority. Service-related calls to action.
**Mix per week.** 3 to 5 posts. Educational local + program update + service offer.

## Website blog and vlog

**Audience.** Search, AI answer engines, downstream of social.
**Role.** Long-form authority and the cited source for AEO and GEO. Blog and vlog topics convert into Reel, Short, GBP, and LinkedIn content the same week.
**Cadence.** 1 to 2 long-form pieces per week. Each one fuels 6 to 10 derivative social posts.

## Cross-platform rules

- One seed never ships as identical copy on two platforms.
- Hashtag posture varies (see `caption-rules.md`).
- CTA varies by audience (see `_shared/knowledge/safe-cta-list.md`).
- Every video script has a paired thumbnail spec and an on-screen text block.
- Personal-brand posts skip GBP and skip LinkedIn unless the moment is leadership-relevant.
