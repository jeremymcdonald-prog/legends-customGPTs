# Daily Content Mix

The weekly rhythm Jeremy holds himself to. The Weekly Production Engine uses this to assign pillars and platforms to each day before the Social Media skill writes anything.

## Weekly minimums

| Channel | Posts per week |
|---|---|
| Facebook Personal | 7 |
| Facebook Business | 7 |
| Instagram Feed or Reel | 7 |
| Instagram Stories | 7 (daily) |
| TikTok | 5 to 7 |
| YouTube Shorts | 5 to 7 |
| YouTube Long Form | 2 |
| LinkedIn | 5 |
| Google Business Profile | 3 to 5 |
| Blog or vlog | 1 to 2 |

## Required weekly story mix

- 2 borrower story posts
- 1 Realtor AI workflow post
- 1 closing or file win post
- 1 personal brand post
- 1 motivational or discipline post

## Default day-to-pillar map (template)

| Day | Lead pillar | Secondary pillar |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Broker advantage | Motivational |
| Tuesday | First-time buyers OR VA | Realtor AI education |
| Wednesday | Mortgage story | Local market authority |
| Thursday | Investor strategy | YouTube long form |
| Friday | Closing day win | Personal brand |
| Saturday | Realtor AI education | Lifestyle / family |
| Sunday | Reflection / leadership | Team authority |

The Weekly Production Engine can override the default per the calendar Jeremy approves for that week.

## Variation rule

No pillar can appear in the lead slot two days in a row. If the engine repeats a pillar, it swaps to a different angle.

## Story slot rule

Story posts go on Wednesday and Friday by default. If a real win lands on a different day, the engine can shift one story slot in that week without breaking the minimums.

## Holiday and event overrides

- Memorial Day, Veterans Day, Independence Day: VA pillar lead.
- Tax filing season: First-time buyer affordability pillar lead.
- Black Friday / cyber week: skip retail promotional framing; lean into broker advantage instead.
- Local Jacksonville events: lean Local SEO pillar that week.
