## Facebook Business

Here is the AI move every Northeast Florida Realtor should know.

Open Claude. Paste your listing details. Tell it three things.

1. The neighborhood story (school, walkability, vibe).
2. The buyer profile.
3. The voice you want (warm, sharp, luxurious, family).

You get a draft in 30 seconds you can edit in 3 minutes. Total listing description time goes from 30 minutes to under 5.

Two rules. Always read every word before posting. Never let it invent facts about the property.
