# The 14 Production Layouts

Every layout: best use, composition, text zones, subject placement, logo, compliance, CTA, mobile safe zones. Mobile safe zone rule throughout: keep all text and the face inside the center 80 percent; keep nothing critical in the outer 10 percent on any edge; assume the bottom 12 percent may be covered by platform UI.

## 1. Hero Portrait + Headline (Engine A/C)
**Best use:** market update, authority, single lesson.
**Composition:** subject right third, dark left two-thirds for a color-blocked text zone.
**Text zones:** headline + one support line + CTA in the left block.
**Subject:** right third, three-quarter body, eye contact or looking into the copy.
**Logo:** bottom-left lockup.
**Compliance:** thin bottom strip.
**CTA:** under the support line.
*Proof: Rate Update.*

## 2. Split Screen Pain vs Solution (Engine B)
**Best use:** bank vs broker, myth vs truth.
**Composition:** vertical or diagonal split, darker/colder "pain" side, warmer/orange "solution" side.
**Text zones:** one label per side, one verdict line across the seam.
**Subject:** on the solution side.
**Logo:** bottom-center.
**Compliance:** bottom strip.
**CTA:** solution side.
**Hard rule:** never depict a named competitor on the pain side.

## 3. Cinematic Office Authority (Engine A)
**Best use:** leadership, growth, team wins.
**Composition:** Jeremy in a dark premium office, Legends wall logo behind.
**Text zones:** top-left headline, optional gold script accent.
**Subject:** center-right, relaxed power pose.
**Logo:** on-set wall + bottom lockup.
**Compliance:** bottom strip.
**CTA:** optional.
*Proof: Friday Momentum.*

## 4. Luxury Command Center (Engine A)
**Best use:** AI authority, tech leadership, "how I work."
**Composition:** Jeremy at a desk, glowing stylized dashboards behind (illustrative, not real client data), city night skyline.
**Text zones:** large left headline, small bottom hook + "read the comments."
**Subject:** seated center-right.
**Logo:** on tee + on screens.
**Compliance:** bottom strip; dashboards must read as illustrative, never as real performance claims.
**CTA:** comment prompt.
*Proof: AI Command Center.*

## 5. Testimonial Proof Card (Engine A)
**Best use:** real reviews only.
**Composition:** dark stage, a single clean review card (real quote, real first name + city), gold stars.
**Text zones:** "What clients say" header, the quote, loan-type tags.
**Subject:** small signature lower-third, not a cartoon mascot.
**Logo:** bottom lockup.
**Compliance:** bottom strip + "Individual results vary. Not a commitment to lend."
**CTA:** optional.
*Replaces the cartoon Testimonial Thursdays.*

## 6. Market Update Card (Engine C)
**Best use:** weekly rate/market note.
**Composition:** color-blocked orange or charcoal panel left, subject right.
**Text zones:** "RATE UPDATE" label, one insight line, DM CTA.
**Subject:** right third.
**Logo:** bottom lockup.
**Compliance:** strip; no rate number unless APR context is added.
**CTA:** DM keyword.
*Proof: Rate Update.*

## 7. Webinar Promo Card (Engine A/C)
**Best use:** webinar registration.
**Composition:** dark stage, title block, date/time chip, register CTA.
**Text zones:** topic title, 3 "you'll learn" bullets, date/time, CTA.
**Subject:** right third or lower-third.
**Logo:** bottom lockup.
**Compliance:** strip + "Educational. Terms and program guidelines apply."
**CTA:** Register link/keyword.

## 8. Realtor Partner Education Card (Engine C)
**Best use:** agent value, co-branded tips.
**Composition:** lighter premium interior allowed here, subject working.
**Text zones:** "REAL ESTATE AGENTS:" hook, 3-4 benefit bullets, CTA.
**Subject:** at laptop/desk.
**Logo:** Legends + partner space.
**Compliance:** strip; RESPA-safe, no thing-of-value-for-referrals language.
**CTA:** "Want the Realtor version?"
*Proof: ChatGPT-for-agents image (upgrade to dark DNA).*

## 9. Loan Program Spotlight (Engine C)
**Best use:** FHA/VA/USDA/DPA/DSCR/refi.
**Composition:** dark stage, program name as the big idea, 3 icon facts.
**Text zones:** program headline, 3 facts, "terms apply" line, CTA.
**Subject:** right third.
**Logo:** bottom lockup.
**Compliance:** strip + "Terms, eligibility, and program guidelines apply." No government endorsement for VA/FHA/USDA.
**CTA:** DM keyword.

## 10. Myth Buster (Engine B/C)
**Best use:** credit, buyer, rate myths.
**Composition:** "MYTH / TRUTH" stacked or split, orange truth accent.
**Text zones:** myth line (struck through), truth line, CTA.
**Subject:** side third.
**Logo:** bottom.
**Compliance:** strip.
**CTA:** comment keyword.
*Proof: Money Moves Monday "credit score isn't Credit Karma."*

## 11. Before & After (Engine B/C)
**Best use:** credit improvement concept, debt consolidation concept, readiness.
**Composition:** left "before" darker, right "after" brighter/orange.
**Text zones:** before label, after label, one outcome line.
**Subject:** optional lower-third.
**Logo:** bottom.
**Compliance:** strip + "Illustrative example. Not a guarantee of results."
**Hard rule:** never show real borrower documents or data.
**CTA:** keyword.

## 12. Checklist Card (Engine C)
**Best use:** buyer readiness, doc list, steps.
**Composition:** dark stage, 4-6 checkmark items in orange, generous spacing.
**Text zones:** title, checklist, CTA.
**Subject:** side third or none.
**Logo:** bottom.
**Compliance:** strip.
**CTA:** "Comment CHECKLIST."

## 13. Closing Celebration (Engine A)
**Best use:** closings, wins, gratitude.
**Composition:** warm cinematic, home or keys motif, celebratory but premium.
**Text zones:** "Closing Day" script, one line, optional stat.
**Subject:** center or with clients (with written permission only; otherwise stylized).
**Logo:** bottom lockup.
**Compliance:** strip; no borrower PII, no real closing docs.
**CTA:** optional.
*Proof: Veteran Closing Day.*

## 14. AI Workflow Authority Post (Engine A)
**Best use:** AI leadership, tools, automation.
**Composition:** command center, stylized tool panels (ChatGPT, Claude, Codex, n8n).
**Text zones:** bold claim headline, support line, comment CTA.
**Subject:** seated authority.
**Logo:** on tee/screens + bottom.
**Compliance:** strip; tools shown as illustrative.
**CTA:** "Read the comments."
*Proof: AI Command Center.*
