# Usage Notes — Weekly Production Engine

## When to invoke

- "Lock Week N."
- "Dispatch Week N production."
- "Show me the tracker for Week N."
- "What's blocking Week N from going live?"
- "Run the Week N performance review."

## The lifecycle one week looks like

1. **Monday morning of prior week.** Engine drafts Week N plan from the 60-day master calendar.
2. **Monday afternoon.** Jeremy approves Week N.
3. **Monday evening.** Engine locks Week N seeds (pillars, platforms, story slots, local angles).
4. **Tuesday.** Story Bank pulls confirmed source IDs. Social Media writes caption packs. Local SEO writes canonical local pieces.
5. **Wednesday.** Canva fills, HeyGen renders, Higgsfield generates.
6. **Thursday.** Operator uploads exports, fills `media_url_tracker.csv`.
7. **Friday.** Engine asks HeroPost Scheduler to promote Week N from staging to final.
8. **Friday or Saturday.** Operator imports final CSV into HeroPost.
9. **Week N runs.**
10. **End of Week N + 24 hours.** Engine captures performance data.
11. **Start of Week N+1 cycle.** Engine reweights based on what worked.

## What the Engine never does

- Produce content directly.
- Override a downstream compliance gate.
- Auto-publish.
- Skip the weekly performance review.

## What you ask the Engine for

- A locked weekly plan.
- A dispatch run.
- A status check.
- A performance review and reweight.
- A bottleneck list.

## Pairing with the AI Boardroom

When a week features a high-risk asset (guarantee creative, named customer, rate quote), the Engine pauses and asks the AI Boardroom to run the Concept Gate before generation proceeds.
