## Strategic outcome

Establish Jeremy as the broker authority for Jacksonville and Northeast Florida, with credible AI Implementation positioning for the Loan Factory CTO candidacy, while building a story-driven content machine that doesn't burn the operator out.

## Three concurrent positioning tracks

1. **Mortgage broker authority** — broker-beats-bank, real stories, real closings, plain spoken education.
2. **Local Northeast Florida authority** — GBP, blog, vlog, AEO surfaces optimized for Jacksonville, Duval, St. Johns, Clay, Nassau.
3. **AI Implementation authority** — Realtor AI tips, leadership content, LinkedIn-leaning posts that double as CTO portfolio evidence.

- Mortgage stories: 14 percent

## Story discipline

- Minimum 2 borrower stories per week.
- Every story has a Story Bank source ID.
- No invented customers, no invented reviews, no invented numbers.
- Anonymized by default; named only with written permission on file.

## Quality bar

- One CTA per piece.
- Compliance footer on every mortgage-related piece.
- No two captions identical across platforms.
- Channel-native voice per `companions/06-social-media/knowledge/platform-output-rules.md`.

## Production discipline

- Plan 60. Lock 1. Ship 1. Reweight next.
- Never produce 60 days of assets at once.
- Higgsfield reserved for premium assets (3 to 6 per week).
- HeyGen for bulk video; Jeremy on camera for personal and high-trust moments.
- Canva for everything text-heavy.

## Success markers (60 day)

- 60 to 70 GBP posts published.
- 18+ YouTube long form videos planned, 8 to 14 published.
- 35+ Reels shipped.
- Story Bank seeded with 30+ source IDs (real, anonymized).
- 10+ blog posts published with schema.
- Performance feedback loop running on a weekly cadence.

## What this 60-day machine deliberately does NOT do

- Auto-publish anything.
- Quote a rate without APR.
- Run a guarantee creative without verified live config.
- Invent customers or testimonials.
- Sit on a single hit and repeat it ten times.
