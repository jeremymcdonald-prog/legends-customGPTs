## Repurposing tree (one piece → many)

A single mortgage story typically powers, over a 60-day window:

- 1 blog post (1100 words, canonical)
- 1 long form video (8 to 12 minutes)
- 1 IG carousel (8 slides)
- 1 IG Reel + TikTok + YT Short (45 seconds)
- 1 IG Story set (4 to 5 frames)
- 1 GBP local post
- 1 LinkedIn leadership angle
- 1 Facebook personal soft mention
- 1 Facebook business mortgage-broker framing

That's 9 derivative pieces from one source over 2 to 4 weeks.

## CTA distribution

- Save / Share: 30 percent of posts.
- Comment keyword: 25 percent.
- DM keyword: 20 percent.
- Call (GBP focus): 10 percent.
- Read the comments / Follow: 10 percent.
- No CTA (pure personal brand): 5 percent.

## Time-of-day defaults (Eastern Time)

Defaults live in `companions/06-social-media/knowledge/usage-notes.md`. Engine can override per day for events.

## What this strategy refuses

- Spray-and-pray hashtag stuffing.
- Stock-photo flyer content.
- Identical captions across platforms.
- Auto-publish.
- Fake reviews or invented numbers.
