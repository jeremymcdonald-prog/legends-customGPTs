# Local SEO, GEO, AEO Strategy

## Target market

Jacksonville and Northeast Florida. Primary counties: Duval, St. Johns, Clay, Nassau. Primary cities listed in `companions/08-local-seo-geo-aeo/knowledge/local-service-area-map.md`.

## Three surfaces

1. **SEO** — Google organic search. Long form blog and vlog pages on mcdonald-mtg.com.
2. **AEO** — Answer engines (AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini). Direct-answer paragraphs, FAQ schema, plain English.
3. **GEO** — Local generative answer surface. Authority signals: NAP consistency, real reviews, structured data, named author entity (Jeremy McDonald).

## 60-day surface plan

| Surface | Target |
|---|---|
| Blog posts | 10 to 12 long form (1100 to 1400 words) |
| Vlog pages | 6 to 9 vlog pages with transcripts |
| GBP posts | 30 to 40 |
| GBP review replies | 100% reply rate on incoming reviews within 72 hours |

## GBP post categories

- Local education (mortgage idea framed for the local market).
- Program update (VA, FHA, USDA, DSCR, FTHB).
- Service offer (one CTA, soft).
- Story-backed lesson (anonymized).
- Market context (sourced).
- Holiday or event (Memorial Day, Veterans Day, Independence Day, tax season, back to school).

## Schema deployment plan

| Page | Schema |
|---|---|
| Home | LocalBusiness + WebSite |
| Service pages (VA, FHA, FTHB, DSCR, USDA, jumbo) | Service + LocalBusiness + FAQPage |
| Each blog post | Article + FAQPage (when FAQ section present) |
| Each vlog page | VideoObject + Article |
| About / team page | Person + Organization |

## Internal linking plan

- Each service page links to 3 to 5 related service pages.
- Each blog post links to 2 to 4 service pages.
- Footer carries NAP, NMLS, EHO, primary service area, sitemap link.
- Author bio links to all service pages.

## What this strategy refuses

- Doorway pages (one page per zip with the same body).
- Hidden text or keyword stuffing.
- Mass AI-generated content without real author voice.
- Invented local stats.
- Fake reviews aggregated into a wall.
