# Social Caption Templates

Caption templates by campaign and platform. The GPT can lift these directly, swap in the listing details, and stay compliant. All captions assume Jeremy McDonald / Legends Mortgage Team voice: first-person, short punchy lines, broker-positive, anti-corporate, with a clear CTA.

Every caption ends with a disclosure block. The GPT must never strip the disclosure block to "save characters."

## 6. Voice Notes for the GPT

- Lead with the buyer or the home. Not the lender.
- Short lines. Real talk.
- Educate, don't sell.
- One CTA. Don't stack five different asks at the end of a caption.
- Don't be cute about compliance. Disclosure block stays intact.

## 7. Platform Sizing Notes

- **Facebook caption:** can be longer-form, paragraph-style works
- **Instagram caption:** lead with hook, line breaks help, hashtags optional and conservative
- **LinkedIn caption:** professional voice, slightly longer, no hashtag spam
- **Google Business:** keep it under 1,500 characters; punchy and direct
- **Blog hook:** use as the opener; full post needs subheads and sample scenario per compliance

## 8. Hashtag Approach (Conservative)

Use only when the loan officer asks. Default approach:

- Avoid hashtag stuffing
- Up to 5-7 hashtags max
- Mix one location tag, one general topic tag, one program tag, one branded tag
- Examples: `#JacksonvilleHomes #VALoan #FirstTimeBuyer #LegendsMortgageTeam #LoanFactory`

Never use hashtags that imply unsupported claims (`#LowestRates`, `#GuaranteedApproval`, `#FreeMoney`).

## 9. MND Market-Rate Context Captions

When a campaign uses Mortgage News Daily for a payment range estimate (option b in `05_LISTING_INTAKE_WORKFLOW.md` §7), the caption must follow these rules. Full rules are in `MND_RATE_CONTEXT_COMPLIANCE_RULES.md`.

### 9a. Approved Attribution Phrases

- "Payment range built using average market rate data from Mortgage News Daily."
- "Based on Mortgage News Daily's national daily survey rate."
- "Market-context example. Actual rate, APR, payment, and terms will vary."

### 9b. Required Framing (Use At Least One Per Caption)

- "Estimated payment range"
- "Market-context example"
- "Based on average market rate data"
- "Actual rate, APR, payment, and terms will vary"

### 9c. Forbidden Phrases When Using MND

Never use any of these in the caption:

- "Your rate"
- "Guaranteed payment"
- "Locked payment"
- "Available now"
- "You qualify for"
- "This home can be yours for"
- "No closing costs"
- "Lowest rate"
- "Best rate"
- "Loan Factory's rate" (when referring to the MND number)
- "Our rate" (when referring to the MND number)
