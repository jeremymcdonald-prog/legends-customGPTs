# What Not to Touch

A quick reference of areas that are off-limits during loan officer setup.
If you find yourself near any of these, stop and message Jeremy.

## In n8n

- ❌ **Workflows tab** — do not open, do not edit, do not duplicate, do not delete, do not activate any workflow.
- ❌ **Activate / Deactivate toggle** — only Jeremy flips it.
- ❌ **Other people's credentials** — do not rename, delete, or share credentials you didn't create.
- ❌ **Variables / Settings / Source Control** — admin-only.
- ❌ **Executions tab** — read-only is fine if you're curious; do not retry, stop, or delete executions.

## In LegendsOS

- ❌ **Admin Center** (`/admin`, `/admin/users`, `/admin/providers`, `/admin/assets`, `/admin/usage`) — owner-only. If your account can see these pages, ask Jeremy before clicking anything.
- ❌ **Provider toggles** in Settings → Providers — these flip live billing and live external actions. Owner-only.
- ❌ **Shared Resources** at `/shared` — read is fine; do not add, edit, or delete shared templates without Jeremy's say-so.
- ❌ **Asset Library deletes** at `/admin/assets` — never delete assets that other people use.
- ❌ **Impersonation** — if your account can impersonate another user, do not click it without explicit instruction.

## In Google Workspace

- ❌ Do not change page admin roles in Meta Business Manager or Google Business Profile.
- ❌ Do not change Drive folder sharing on team folders.
- ❌ Do not delete shared calendars.

## In any browser tab

- ❌ Do not paste API keys, tokens, or OAuth codes anywhere except their official credential field.
- ❌ Do not stay signed in to a team account on a public computer.
- ❌ Do not install browser extensions that read your tabs while you're in n8n or LegendsOS.

## In ChatGPT (including this GPT)

- ❌ Do not paste a borrower's loan file.
- ❌ Do not paste real client PII (SSN, DOB, full account numbers, full DOBs).
- ❌ Do not paste any password or token.
- ❌ Do not ask this GPT to publish, send, or activate anything.

## Two-line summary

Touch your own credentials. Don't touch workflows. Don't touch admin. Don't paste secrets.
