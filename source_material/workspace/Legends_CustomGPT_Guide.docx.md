# Legends Mortgage Team – CustomGPT Guide

# **Intro**

Welcome to the Legends Mortgage Team’s CustomGPT Guide. These AI assistants were built to give you leverage in every part of the mortgage and real estate process. From structuring loans to writing Instagram captions, they save you time, sharpen your marketing, and give you answers fast.

Think of this as your playbook. Each GPT is specialized, so instead of using a generic chatbot, you’re using a finely tuned assistant designed for your role and your goals.

# **How to Use This Guide**

1\. Find the GPT you need – Each section has the GPT’s name, a quick icon, and a clickable link.

2\. Open it up – Click the link and ChatGPT will open that assistant.

3\. Ask naturally – Type your question or request just like you would with me or a teammate.

4\. Follow the format – Each GPT card explains what it’s for, what it does, when to use it, how to use it, and how it benefits you.

# **Quick Start: Setting Up ChatGPT**

\- Go to https://chat.openai.com

\- Create a free account (or log in if you already have one)

\- Bookmark the links in this guide for quick access

\- On first open, click “Start Chatting” and you’re ready to go
