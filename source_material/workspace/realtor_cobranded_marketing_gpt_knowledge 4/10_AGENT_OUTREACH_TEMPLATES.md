# Agent Outreach Templates — Email, Text, DM

Reusable starter templates for the GPT to drop into outputs. Each template is meant to be lightly customized to the specific listing, agent, and loan officer. Compliance language is non-negotiable.

All templates assume the loan officer sending is on the Legends Mortgage Team and uses their @loanfactory.com email.

## 8. Hard Rules for Outreach

- Use the loan officer's @loanfactory.com email for any agent communication.
- Never imply a referral exchange.
- Never invent agent names, brokerage names, license numbers, or headshots. Agent license numbers are not required and do not block drafts; web-search if practical, leave out if not found.
- Never quote a rate without an APR.
- Never use "guaranteed," "best," "lowest," or "free."
- For agents in WA or listings in WA, omit the $2,000 Best Price Guarantee.
- Always treat outreach copy as draft until compliance has reviewed the version you intend to publish on your channels.
