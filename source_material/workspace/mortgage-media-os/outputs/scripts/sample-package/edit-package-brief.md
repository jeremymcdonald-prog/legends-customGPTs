- Duration: 10-15 seconds overlay on avatar footage

  1. "Get Jeremy's Loan Estimate"

## Music Guidelines

If using background music:
- Volume: -20dB to -25dB (barely audible, should not compete with voice)
- Genre: Subtle corporate/ambient, no lyrics
- License: Must be royalty-free (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or YouTube Audio Library)
- Recommended: Low, warm, confident tone. Not upbeat or cheerful. Think authority, not celebration.
