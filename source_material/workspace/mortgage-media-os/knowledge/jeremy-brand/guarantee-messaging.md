## The Core Guarantee Statement

"I'm so confident that I can find you the lowest available rate and fees for your mortgage that I back it with a $1,000 guarantee. Shop your bank first. Get their best offer in writing. If I can't beat it on both rate AND fees, I'll write you a check for $1,000."

---

## Why The Guarantee Works (The Psychology)

**It reverses the risk.** Normally the consumer bears all the risk in a financial transaction — they share their financial information, apply for a loan, and hope the result is competitive. The guarantee flips that entirely. Now the lender bears the risk of the comparison.

**It signals confidence.** You don't offer a $1,000 guarantee if you're not confident you can deliver. The very existence of the guarantee communicates "I know I'm better, and I'm willing to put money on it."

**It stops the shopping paralysis.** Many buyers don't call me because they assume comparing lenders is complicated or awkward. The guarantee reframes the comparison as simple: get one offer in writing, call me, I'll beat it or pay you $1,000.

**It creates urgency without pressure.** The guarantee creates a reason to act without pressure. "Bring me your best offer and let me compete" feels collaborative, not pushy.
