# Social Image Prompt Library

Battle-tested prompt patterns by post type. Each pattern is structured
the same way: platform, aspect, visual concept, subject, background,
style, text overlay, negative prompt, compliance notes, caption angle.

## First-Time Buyer Celebration

- **Platform:** Instagram feed
- **Aspect:** 4:5
- **Visual concept:** Couple holding house keys in warm golden-hour light, soft front porch in background.
- **Main subject:** Young diverse couple, smiling, hands holding a brass key together.
- **Background:** Suburban front porch at golden hour, slightly blurred.
- **Style:** Lifestyle photographic, shallow depth of field, warm tone, professional.
- **Text overlay:** "Welcome home." Bottom-left, small, white sans-serif. NMLS line bottom-right, smaller still.
- **Negative prompt:** No fake brand logos, no readable rates, no extra fingers, no warped faces, no celebrity likenesses.
- **Compliance notes:** Include NMLS line. No rate/payment numbers in this image. Caption can mention "let's see what fits."
- **Caption angle:** "Day one of forever in this house. Proud to help [first names] make it happen."

## Realtor Team-Up (Co-Branded)

- **Platform:** Facebook feed
- **Aspect:** 4:5
- **Visual concept:** Two professionals side-by-side, hands shaking, behind them a clean modern home exterior. Subtle gold "+" symbol between them.
- **Main subject:** LO on left, Realtor on right (use stand-in if no headshot). Both professional, smiling.
- **Background:** Modern home exterior, daylight, clean composition.
- **Style:** Editorial photographic, balanced lighting, professional. Champagne gold geometric accent line across the bottom.
- **Text overlay:** "Partners in the close." Top-left. NMLS line bottom-right small.
- **Negative prompt:** No fake brand logos, no fake brokerage names, no fake property addresses.
- **Compliance notes:** Include NMLS line. No rate claims. Don't imply Realtor receives anything of value for the referral.
- **Caption angle:** "Lending + listing, same team. Tag the Realtor you want to close more deals with."

## Refinance Question (Carousel Cover)

- **Platform:** Instagram feed (carousel)
- **Aspect:** 1:1
- **Visual concept:** Dark charcoal background with a single gold question mark made of subtle geometric lines. Small house silhouette inside the curve of the question mark.
- **Main subject:** Stylized gold question mark with house silhouette.
- **Background:** Solid dark charcoal with faint glass texture.
- **Style:** Editorial illustrated, premium dark-gold-glass aesthetic.
- **Text overlay:** "Refinance — yes? No? Maybe?" Centered, large, white. NMLS line bottom-center small.
- **Negative prompt:** No rate numbers visible, no fake bank logos.
- **Compliance notes:** If next carousel slides cite rates, APR must be equally prominent. Avoid "save $X/month."
- **Caption angle:** "Three questions to decide if a refi makes sense right now. Save this carousel for later."

## Listing Marketing (Just Listed)

- **Platform:** Facebook feed
- **Aspect:** 4:5
- **Visual concept:** Beautiful home exterior, golden hour, "Just Listed" gold ribbon banner across the top corner.
- **Main subject:** The home (LO will provide reference photo).
- **Background:** The actual listing's location.
- **Style:** Lifestyle photographic with one premium dark-gold-glass banner overlay top-left.
- **Text overlay:** "Just Listed." Top-left in gold ribbon. Address optional bottom-left small. NMLS line bottom-right.
- **Negative prompt:** No price numbers in the image (price in caption only), no fake addresses, no readable fake signage.
- **Compliance notes:** This is real estate marketing, not loan marketing — the loan marketing rules don't apply. But if the post mentions financing options, the NMLS line and APR rules come back in.
- **Caption angle:** "Just listed in [neighborhood]. [X] bed [X] bath, [feature]. DM for the full tour."

## Investor / DSCR / BRRRR

- **Platform:** Instagram feed
- **Aspect:** 1:1
- **Visual concept:** Modern duplex or small multifamily exterior, gold overlay arrows representing flow (rent → cash flow).
- **Main subject:** The investment property.
- **Background:** Daytime urban or suburban, clean.
- **Style:** Editorial photographic + subtle infographic overlay, dark-gold glass accents.
- **Text overlay:** "Cash-flow plays." Top-left. NMLS line bottom-right.
- **Negative prompt:** No fake brokerage logos, no fake cap-rate percentages visible.
- **Compliance notes:** NMLS line required. Don't claim guaranteed returns. Don't post specific dollar projections.
- **Caption angle:** "Investor financing isn't one-size-fits-all. DSCR, BRRRR, conventional — let's match the strategy to the deal."

## Google Business Profile Update

- **Platform:** Google Business Profile post
- **Aspect:** 1:1
- **Visual concept:** Single bold concept tied to a current month/week theme (e.g., May = home-buying season).
- **Main subject:** Big central icon (house, key, calendar, etc.) on Legends dark-gold-glass background.
- **Background:** Solid charcoal with subtle gold geometric pattern.
- **Style:** Editorial clean, single focal point, easy to read in a feed.
- **Text overlay:** Short headline (3-5 words). NMLS line bottom-center.
- **Negative prompt:** No rates, no fake logos.
- **Compliance notes:** Standard NMLS line. Don't post rate numbers on GBP.
- **Caption angle:** "This week we're seeing [trend]. Quick scenario? Call or DM."

## YouTube Thumbnail

- **Platform:** YouTube
- **Aspect:** 16:9
- **Visual concept:** High-contrast face shot (use LO's headshot when supplied) + big bold hook text + one clear visual element.
- **Main subject:** LO headshot (left third) + bold hook text (right two-thirds).
- **Background:** Dark gradient charcoal → black, subtle gold light streak.
- **Style:** Editorial cinematic, high contrast, readable at thumbnail size.
- **Text overlay:** The hook (e.g., "Refi Math 101"), 6-8 words max, huge, white with gold accent on key word. NMLS line bottom-right small.
- **Negative prompt:** No clutter, no extra text, no fake logos.
- **Compliance notes:** Standard NMLS line. If the video discusses rates, the video itself must show APR — the thumbnail doesn't have to.
- **Caption angle:** N/A — thumbnails don't get captions. Video description handles disclosures.

## Use this library

When an LO asks for a post type, pull the matching pattern, swap in their specifics, and hand it back as a single structured prompt.
