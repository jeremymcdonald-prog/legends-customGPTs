## Operating rules

- Read `01_SOURCE_OF_TRUTH.md` before doing work.

- Keep scope tight.
- Leave a handoff after every work block.

## Required output

At the end of your pass, update:

- `STATUS.md`
- `HANDOFF.md`
- `NEXT_ACTIONS.md`

## Do not do

- Do not spend money.
- Do not send emails.
- Do not publish social posts.
- Do not expose secrets.
- Do not change production without approval.
