# LegendsOS — Vision

## What is LegendsOS?

LegendsOS is the AI command center for The Legends Mortgage Team. It gives
every team member — loan officers, processors, coordinators, and marketing —
one place to work with AI assistants, create content, manage knowledge, and
execute their daily workflows.

## Core belief

Mortgage teams spend too much time switching between tools, copying data, and
doing repetitive work. AI should handle the repetition so people can focus on
relationships and judgment.

## Product pillars

1. **Atlas** — AI assistant with loan context, knowledge retrieval, and tool use
2. **Studios** — Social, email, and image creation with compliance awareness
3. **Training** — Team education via video, coaching, and structured academy tracks
4. **Automation** — n8n-powered workflows for publishing, email, and intake
5. **Admin** — Owner oversight of users, usage, connections, and security

## Target users

- Jeremy McDonald (owner) — full platform access, builder tools, GOAT API
- Loan officers — Atlas, marketing assistant, My Loans, resources
- Processors — FLO processing cockpit, document management
- Coordinators — Follow-up board, borrower communication
- Marketing team — Studios, calendar, content planning

## Success criteria

- Team members use LegendsOS daily instead of switching between 5+ tools
- Content creation time drops by 60%+
- Training completion and coaching engagement are measurable
- Owner has full visibility into team activity and AI usage
